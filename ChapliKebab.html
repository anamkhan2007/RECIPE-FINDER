Chapli Kebab

Spicy, juicy, and crispy kebabs from the heart of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa 🇵🇰

⏱️ Cooking Time

  • Preparation: 20 minutes
  • Marination (optional): 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 20–25 minutes

🍽️ Nutrition (Per Kebab)

  • Calories: ~280 kcal
  • Protein: ~18g
  • Fat: ~20g
  • Carbs: ~6g

🧂 Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef or mutton (with fat)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp crushed coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg (for binding)
  • 2 tbsp cornmeal or gram flour (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Oil for shallow frying

🔥 Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the minced meat with onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add all the spices, egg, chopped coriander, and cornmeal for binding.
  3. Mix well and refrigerate for 20–30 minutes for better shape.
  4. Take small portions and flatten into round, thick patties (about palm size).
  5. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry kebabs on medium heat for 4–5 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with naan, chutney, and sliced onions or salad.
Tip: Traditional Chapli Kebabs include chopped tomatoes inside the mixture, which gives them juiciness but may cause breakage — handle gently while frying.
