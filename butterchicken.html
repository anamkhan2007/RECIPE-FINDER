Butter Chicken

Rich, creamy, mildly spiced North Indian chicken dish

⏱️ Cooking Time

  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Cook Time: 40 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hour

🍛 Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~450–500 kcal
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 25g
  • Carbs: 20g

🧂 Ingredients

For Marinating Chicken:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into pieces)
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1½ tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste

For Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup or paste
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix chicken with marinade ingredients. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight).
  2. Grill or sauté the marinated chicken in a pan until fully cooked. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, heat butter and oil. Sauté chopped onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell goes away.
  5. Add tomato puree, ketchup, turmeric, red chili, and garam masala. Cook until oil separates.
  6. Stir in cream and sugar, then gently add cooked chicken. Mix well and simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and a swirl of cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.
Chef’s Tip: Use smoked paprika for a richer color and slight smokiness, or grill the chicken on charcoal for authentic flavor!
