Khaddi Kebab

Traditional underground whole lamb roast from Balochistan, Pakistan

⏱️ Cooking Time

  • Preparation Time: 1 hour
  • Marination Time: 6–8 hours
  • Cooking Time: 4–6 hours (underground pit)

🍽️ Nutrition (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~600–800 kcal
  • Protein: High (lamb-based)
  • Fat: Depends on cut
  • Carbs: Low

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1 whole lamb or goat (8–12 kg, cleaned)
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 tbsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp black pepper (coarsely ground)
  • 2 tbsp cumin (roasted & crushed)
  • Optional: chili flakes, coriander powder
  • Charcoal, iron rods, banana leaves or foil

🔥 Instructions

  1. Clean and wash the lamb thoroughly, including inside the cavity.
  2. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and all spices into a marinade.
  3. Rub marinade generously all over and inside the lamb. Marinate for 6–8 hours or overnight.
  4. Dig a pit in the ground and burn wood or charcoal until it's full of red-hot coals.
  5. Insert iron rod through the lamb to suspend it. Wrap the lamb in banana leaves or foil.
  6. Place the lamb inside the pit, cover with lid or steel sheet, and seal edges with mud.
  7. Let it cook underground with the trapped heat for 4–6 hours.
  8. Unseal, remove lamb carefully, and serve hot with naan or rice.
Traditional Tip: Authentic Khaddi Kebab has no added oil or ghee. The meat roasts in its own juices, infused with smoky charcoal aroma. Best enjoyed fresh and hot.
