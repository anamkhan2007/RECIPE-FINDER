Aloo Tuk

Crispy double-fried Sindhi-style potatoes with bold spices

⏱️ Cooking Time

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 30 minutes

🍽️ Serves

  • 2 to 3 people
  • Great side dish or snack

🧂 Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes (peeled and sliced into thick rounds)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp dry mango powder (amchur) or lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thick round slices (about ½ inch).
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry the potatoes until just soft (not crispy). Remove and let them cool.
  4. Press each slice gently to flatten.
  5. Fry them again on high heat until golden and crispy.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. While hot, sprinkle all the spices and salt evenly.
  8. Toss gently and serve hot and crispy!
Tip: For extra crunch, chill the potatoes for 10–15 minutes between the first and second fry!
